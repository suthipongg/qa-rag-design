This ergonomic office chair has lots of functions that allow you to easily adapt the chair to your body.

Lumbar support – Promotes good posture and reduces strain on lower back.

Armrests – Support your forearms and reduce strain on the back and shoulders.

Automatic tension – Adjusts the resistance of the backrest based on your body weight.

Synchronised tilt tension – Moves with you as you recline, opening the hips and torso to improve circulation and energy.

Tilt Lock – Locks the chair in an upright position.

Height adjustable seat – For a comfortable sitting position regardless of body height.

Washable – Removable covers make cleaning easy and keep the chair fresh.

Safety function – Castors lock when the chair is not in use to keep it securely in place.
